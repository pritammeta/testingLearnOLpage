Features

Feature 1

Interactive Classroom

Experience LIVE learning and real-time demonstrations.
Our virtual platform helps you conduct face-to-face seamless interactions with students. With live comments and real-time project demos, online learning becomes more collaborative. Students joining in late get a fair chance to attend each session. Flexible program structuring ensures all sessions are in sync with student and teacher schedules.


Feature 2

Coursework Management

Organize and ascribe assignments conveniently.
With smooth online connectivity, program coursework gets shared easily among students. Teachers can create and allocate assignments from anywhere and at any time. The platform enables flexible grading and instant result generation for tests. Students get custom-made digital study materials, which can be organized and filtered by date, syllabus, complexity levels, and programs.
Feature 3

Responsive Discussions

Generate rich feedback with productive classroom debates.
Our built-in tools support asynchronous online discussions and spontaneous dialogue between students and teachers. The LearnOL Discussion Board makes teaching more productive with its smart whiteboard that facilitates constructive classroom conversations. Students can share their ideas through real-time feedback. Active brainstorming makes the live learning sessions highly engaging..
Feature 4

Time Management

Invest more time in educating than organizing.
LearnOL lets teachers tackle all their tasks promptly. With its mobility features, the faculty can conduct their sessions anywhere, anytime, and on any device. Flexible scheduling helps students learn conveniently and they can skip or revisit chapters with bi-directional coursework navigation. Our platform also allows users to set reminders for webinars and live sessions and encourage punctual attendance.
Feature 5

Classroom Administration

Run a hassle-free online classroom.
The best part for teachers and program administrators is the ability to manage classrooms effectively. The platform is equipped with student attendance management tools that help teachers assess their learners better. By setting up clear grading instructions for every task, student performance evaluation becomes more systematic. They can also maintain a records book on homework and coursework for building transparent student reviews.
Feature 6

Privacy and Security

Protect your quality education with authentic access control.
Educators can securely store their coursework, tests, and assignments on the platform. Our data security algorithms ensure no student or third-party gets unauthorized access to your resources. The platform also maintains the data privacy of confidential information on teachers, students, their guardians, and learning partners.
